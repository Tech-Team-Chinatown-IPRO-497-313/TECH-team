Pui Tak Center(培德大厦)

History

Established in 1893, On Leong Merchants Association was one of the biggest Chinese organization aimed to help with Chinese immigration. In Chinese, On Leong means peaceful. They helped Chinese community settle down in Chicago area and opened Chinese language school for children. In order to support the develop of the association, On Leong Tong invited architects Christian S. Michaelsen and Sigurd A. Rognstad to built the On Leong Merchants Association Building in 1928. However, because of the increasing power of the Chinese gang tongs, the On Leong Merchants Association Building become the center of crime and casino. In 1988 FBI raided the building, shut down all the casinos and took charge of it. In 1993, the Chinese Christian Union Church purchased the former On Leong Merchant Association Building from the federal government and changed its name to Pui Tak Center. In Chinese ‘Pui Tak’ means to build a polite and friendly character or culture. The newly named Pui Tak Center now hosts various religious, community, and educational programs, such as English-as-a-Second-Language courses (ESL).

历史

1893年建立的梁商协会是中国最大的一个致力于帮助中国移民的组织。在中文中，安良的意思是和平。他们帮助华人社区在芝加哥地区定居，并为儿童开办了中文学校。为了支持协会的发展，安良堂邀请建筑师Christian S. Michaelsen 和 Sigurd A. Rognstad 在1928年建造了梁家商协会大楼。然而，由于中国帮派火钳的力量日益增强，梁家商协会大楼成为犯罪和赌场的中心。1988年，联邦调查局袭击了这座大楼，关闭了所有赌场并负责处理。 1993年，中国基督教联合会从联邦政府购买了前安良商会协会大楼，并更名为培德中心。在中文里 “培德” 意味着建立一个礼貌友好的人格或文化。新命名的培德中心现在举办各种宗教、社区和教育课程，如英语作为第二语言课程（ESL）。

Architecture

When On Leong planed to build On Leong Merchants Association Building, there are no Chinese licensed architect in Illinois, one of the association’s leaders had previously worked with Michaelsen and Rognstad, and recommended the firm. At that time there were no Chinese architecture around, so the architecture can only learn traditional Chinese buildings through books. As a result, their design is not following the rules of Chinese traditional architecture.

建筑

当安良计划建立安良商会大厦时，当时的伊利诺伊州没有中国注册建筑师，该协会的一位领导人之前曾与 Michaelsen 和 Rognstad 合作，并推荐了该公司。当时，周围没有中式建筑，所以建筑只能通过书本学习中国传统建筑。因此，他们的设计并没有遵循中国传统建筑的规则。

Architecture Facts

Its basic form is influenced by the Chinese Theater built during the World’s Columbian Exposition in 1893 in Chicago, and the Chinese theater shaped from religious architecture called ‘Bao Ta’, ‘Bao Ta’ originated from India, it is the place where people bury the ashes of buddha after he die. As Buddhism spread to China, you can also see ‘Bao Ta’ in some temples in south of China. Usually the level of ‘Bao Ta’ in China is usually odd because of the theory of ‘Yin and Yang’ and they are always a complete and separate building. But the Chinese Theater and On Leong Merchants Association Building are both even story and are only used on the top of the buildings.


In Chinese traditional buildings. Stone lions are the guard of the buildings and they are usually put in front of the main entrance of building, instead the lions become the ornamentation on the walls of second floor in On Leong Merchants Association Building.


You will never see peacock as decoration in any kind of chinese traditional buildings, they usually put phoenix or dragon as a symbol to protect people. Instead around 1910s in Europe and the US, peacock was used a lot in the Art Nouveau architecture style as the symbol of nature and fortune. So Michaelsen and Rognstad probably was also affected by Art Nouveau style.


The facade of On Leong Merchants Association Building looks similar to ‘Qilou’ architecture in Guangzhou, Guangdong province. ‘Qilou’ is called arcade-house in western architecture, it originate from Europe. Guangdong province is one of the first places in China to open to western culture, so ‘Qilou’ emerged from the Guangdong first. Most of ‘Qilou’ are usually two or three stories with protruding arcade in the first floor. Arcades were linked together to form the sidewalk, on the one side is usually commercial center on the other side is the road for cars. And the rest of the building are usually apartments. This design keep the pedestrians safe from cars and rains and also undertake commercial and residential function in city. Strictly speaking, ‘Qilou’ is not traditional Chinese architecture but the blend of Chinese and western architecture style.


风格

它的基本形式受到1893年芝加哥世界哥伦布博览会期间建造的中国戏院的影响，中国剧院由宗教建筑形成，称为“宝塔”，“宝塔”源自印度，是人们埋葬灰烬的地方他死后的佛陀。随着佛教传入中国，您还可以在中国南方的一些寺庙看到“宝塔”。通常，由于“阴阳”理论，“宝塔”在中国通常的单数的楼层，它们一直是一个完整独立的建筑。但中国剧院和安良商会协会大厦都是双数的楼层建筑，而且宝塔的部分仅仅在建筑的顶部。


在中国传统建筑中，石狮子通常被放在建筑物正门前，是建筑物的卫兵，而在这里狮子则成为二楼墙壁上的装饰物。


在任何一种中国的传统建筑中，都不会看到孔雀的装饰，他们通常把凤凰或龙作为保护人类的象征。大约在20世纪10年代在欧洲和美国，孔雀在新艺术主义建筑风格中被用作自然和财富的象征。所以Michaelsen和Rognstad可能也受到新艺术风格的影响。


安良招商会大厦的外观与广东广州的齐楼建筑类似，“齐楼”在西方建筑中被称为拱廊屋，起源于欧洲。广东省是中国第一批向西方开放文化的地区之一，所以'齐楼'首先从广东出现。 “齐楼”大部分通常是二楼或三楼，一楼有拱形拱廊。拱廊连接在一起形成人行道，一边是商业中心，另一边是汽车的道路。而建筑物的其余部分通常是公寓。这种设计可以保护行人免受汽车和雨淋的侵害，并且还能作为城市的商业和住宅功能。严格来说，“齐楼”不是中国传统建筑，而是中西合璧的建筑风格。
