Ping Tom Park（谭继平纪念公园）

Description

Ping Tom Memorial Park located along the edge of the South Branch of the Chicago River in the Armour Square Community. The park was named in honor of the leading force behind its creation, Chinatown's most noted civic leader, Ping Tom (1935-1995). The park's fieldhouse, named in honor of the late Advisory Council President Leonard Louie, was completed in October 2013.


Outside, the park offers an athletic field, walking paths by the river, and a boat house, which is open for kayak rentals during the summer season. The boathouse includes a public dock that is available for use by non-motorized boats during park hours. Outside facilities also include a children's playground and a pagoda-style pavilion.  The park is a popular destination for special events and many of its spaces are available for rental.

简介

谭继平纪念公园位于芝加哥河南岸的阿莫尔广场社区边缘。 这个公园的名字是为了纪念唐人街最着名的公民领袖谭继平（1935-1995）。 一开始，谭继平纪念公园公的前身为于2013年10月份建成的Leonard Louie纪念公园。


在公园外面，公园设有一个运动场，河边的步行道以及一个在夏季开放供皮艇出租的船屋。 船屋包括一个公共码头，在公园时间可供非机动船使用。室外设施还包括儿童游乐场和宝塔式凉亭。 谭继平公园是还是特殊活动的热门目的地，其许多空间可供出租。

History

The Chicago Park District acquired the site for Ping Tom Memorial Park in 1991. Ping Tom Memorial Park's 12-acre site was originally a Chicago and Western Indiana Railroad yard located along the edge of the South Branch of the Chicago River. In 1998, the Chicago Park District began transforming the old rail-yard into a beautiful rolling green space, taking full advantage of impressive river views. In 2002, the Chicago Park District acquired 5 additional acres on the northeast side of the park. Later of 2002, landscape improvements to the expanded area of Ping Tom Memorial Park will soon follow.

历史

1991年，芝加哥公园区获得了谭继平纪念公园的网站。 谭继平纪念公园最初的12英亩地是位于芝加哥河南岸边缘的芝加哥和西印第安纳铁路场地。 1998年，芝加哥公园区开始将旧铁路场改造成一个美丽的起伏绿地，充分利用令人印象深刻的河景。 2002年，谭继平公园区在公园东北侧再获得5英亩土地。2002年下半年，谭继平纪念公园扩建区域的景观改造将很快展开。

Something about Ping Tom

Ping Tom formed the Chinese American Development Corporation in 1984. The private real estate firm transformed a 32-acre rail yard site into Chinatown Square, a $100 million dollar residential and commercial expansion of Chinatown. Active in numerous prominent civic and cultural institutions, Ping Tom was also an advisor to U.S. senators, Illinois governors, and Chicago mayors.

关于谭继平

谭继平于1984年成立了华美发展公司。 这家私人房地产公司将一座32英亩的铁路车场改建为中国城广场，这是一项价值1亿美元的唐人街住宅和商业扩张。 除此之外，谭继平在许多着名的公民和文化机构中活跃，同时他也是美国参议员，伊利诺伊州州长和芝加哥市长的顾问。
